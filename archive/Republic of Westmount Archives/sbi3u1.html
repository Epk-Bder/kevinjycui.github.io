Ryan W 28-Apr-19 09:26 PM
Polygenics: Show continuous variation, control expression of a trait by several genes, Multifactorial traits: control of the expression of the trait by several genes and environmental factors, Epistasis: one gene relies on another gene for it to be expressed.

Ryan W 28-Apr-19 09:27 PM
Autosomal dominant: M and F equally likely to become affected, usually do not skip generations, Autosomal recessive: M and F equally likely to become affected, often skip generation, unaffected parents may have affected children, X-linked dominant: F or an affected M will be affected, no father to son transmission, X-linked recessive: M affected>F, No father to son transmission, Y-linked: all sons of an affected father will be affected.
