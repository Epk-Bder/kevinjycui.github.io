The train to Grande Capital was about an hour's ride from the edge of the metropolitan area, though the recent renovations on the East line meant that a lot more commuters will be taking it.
The city was going through a campaign of construction, as the new party leader of the municipality was determined in improving the city's mostly rustic and outdated infrastructure.
Even outside the local station, you could see a sectioned off side of the road and a band of workers laying out the foundations for a public area of some sort.
